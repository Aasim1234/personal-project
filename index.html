Frequently Asked Questions

What is NEET counseling?
NEET counseling is the process by which students are allotted medical colleges based on their NEET scores through official online platforms.
When does counseling start?
Counseling usually starts after NEET results are declared. The timeline varies by state and MCC.
How can Medicare Consultancy help?
We guide students step-by-step, assist with registration, choice filling, and provide mock counseling sessions to boost confidence.
